AI Ecosystem

EVERY AI

I developed EveryAI to eliminate the friction of managing multiple AI subscriptions. The goal was to create a unified "operating system" for artificial intelligence where users can access over 100+ premium models from a single, streamlined dashboard.

The Platform

By leveraging a Vibe Coding workflow, I focused on the "vibe" and flow of the user experience rather than getting bogged down in boilerplate code—allowing for rapid prototyping of complex AI orchestration.

Key Features

Automated Tool Selection

Users don't need to pick a model; they simply state their goal, and the platform automatically selects the best AI for the job based on real-time requirements.

26-in-1 Marketing Suite

Bundled a comprehensive set of tools for lead generation, traffic automation, and sales funnel construction in a single cloud-based environment.

Commercial-Ready Output

Built specifically for agencies, providing commercial rights for all AI-generated content, branding kits, and sales videos.

Zero-Subscription Model

Engineered to support a lifetime-access approach, removing the need for users to pay monthly fees for individual premium AI services.
