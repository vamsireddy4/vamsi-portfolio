AGENT PRO

An all-in-one AI communication platform built for real estate companies — integrating Voice AI, SMS, WhatsApp & Email campaigns into a single command center.

The Brief

Real estate agencies lose leads every day due to slow follow-ups and fragmented communication tools. Agent Pro brings AI into every touchpoint — from the first call to the signed contract.

Communication Channels

Voice AI

AI-powered inbound & outbound calling that qualifies leads, schedules property viewings, and handles FAQs — all with human-like conversation at sub-300ms latency.

  • 24/7 AI Calling Agent
  • CRM-Synced Call Logging
  • Appointment Booking via Voice

SMS Campaigns

High-volume, personalized SMS outreach to prospects. Automated follow-ups trigger based on lead behaviour — ensuring no lead goes cold.

  • Two-Way AI SMS Replies
  • Delivery & Open Tracking

WhatsApp

Rich-media property listings, virtual tour links, and instant AI auto-replies via Meta-approved templates — reaching buyers where they already are.

  • Meta Template Campaigns
  • Webhook AI Auto-Replies
  • Audience Segmentation

Email Campaigns

AI-written, personalized email sequences for new listings, open houses, and buyer nurture — with full analytics and A/B testing.

  • AI-Drafted Sequences
  • Open & Click Analytics
  • CRM-Triggered Automations

Real Estate AI Capabilities

/ Lead Qualification AI

Automatically scores and qualifies inbound leads based on budget, location preference, and buying timeline — routing hot leads to agents instantly.

/ Property Matching Engine

AI cross-matches prospect requirements against active listings and sends personalized property recommendations across all channels simultaneously.

/ Viewing Scheduler

Voice & chat AI autonomously books property viewings, syncing with agent calendars via Google Calendar and Cal.com with zero manual intervention.

/ Unified Inbox

All SMS, WhatsApp, email, and call transcripts consolidated in one dashboard — giving agents full conversation history without switching apps.

/ AI Follow-Up Sequences

Behaviour-triggered multi-channel follow-up flows that re-engage cold leads at the right time with the right message on the right channel.

/ Analytics Dashboard

Real-time performance metrics across all channels — response rates, booking conversions, campaign ROI, and agent performance in a single view.
