Current Venture

MAGICTEAMS AI

I architected and developed MagicTeams, a high-performance, multi-channel AI operations platform designed to consolidate complex communication workflows into a single "command center". Unlike standard chatbots, this platform serves as an orchestration layer for real-world infrastructure across voice, SMS, WhatsApp, and email.

The Mission

I built the application as a "five-in-one" shell, featuring dedicated workspaces for different business needs—serving as an orchestration layer for real-world infrastructure.

Product Ecosystem

MAGICTEAMS VOICE

A low-latency telephony system for inbound/outbound AI calling, integrated with Twilio and Telnyx.

MAGICTEAMS SMS & GMAIL

Automated outreach modules for high-volume, template-driven campaigns with full delivery and history tracking.

MAGICTEAMS WHATSAPP

A specialized workspace supporting Meta template parsing, audience segmentation, and webhook-based AI auto-replies.

MAGICTEAMS TRAINING

A sales-enablement product (Firebrand) for AI roleplay scenarios with automated scoring based on BANT and SPIN frameworks.
