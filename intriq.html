FinTech Application

INTRIQ CALCULATOR

The Intriq Calculator is a premium financial utility designed to simplify complex interest computations, focusing on delivering a high-end user experience with professional-grade accuracy.

Developer Vision

Developed using a Vibe Coding approach with Xcode and Claude, the app translates complex financial logic into a polished iOS application by focusing on flow and user interaction.

Core Functionality

Simple & Compound

Switch instantly between calculation modes to estimate loan costs or long-term investment growth.

Flexible Frequencies

Calculate compounding on an annual, semi-annual, quarterly, or monthly basis with precision.

Smart History

Auto-saves calculations for easy tracking, with an auto-expiration feature that keeps the interface clean.

Premium UX & Privacy

Refined Interface

Professional color palette with smooth animations and full support for both Light and Dark modes.

Local Storage

All financial data and calculations are stored locally on your device. Your information never leaves your phone.

Use Cases

Built specifically for planning loans, tracking savings, or estimating investment returns with professional clarity.

Developer Context

iOS Engineering

As a technical lead, I built this application using a MacBook Air M4. By utilizing Vibe Coding techniques, I was able to translate complex financial logic into a polished iOS application, leveraging expertise in Xcode and AI-assisted development.
